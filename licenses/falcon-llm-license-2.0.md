## Summary

The Falcon LLM License 2.0 is a custom permissive license issued by Technology
Innovation Institute (TII), the UAE-based research organization that
trained the Falcon family of models. The license:

- **Allows commercial use, modification, and redistribution** of the
  Falcon model weights and derivative works.
- **Requires preserving the copyright notice and attribution** to TII
  in source and derivative materials.
- **Imposes an acceptable-use policy** prohibiting deployment for
  unlawful, harmful, or discriminatory purposes. Specifically the
  policy disallows use in weapons systems, surveillance against
  protected populations, defamation, and generation of CSAM or
  similar abuse content.
- **Disclaims warranties and liability** typical of permissive
  open-source licenses.

The license is materially similar in spirit to Llama Community licenses
in that it pairs broad commercial-use grants with an acceptable-use
policy, but unlike Meta's license it does not impose a monthly-active-user
threshold for additional licensing. Refer to the canonical URL above
for the exact terms.
