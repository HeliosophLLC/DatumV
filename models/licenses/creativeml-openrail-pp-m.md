## Relationship to OpenRAIL-M

OpenRAIL++-M is a derivative of [CreativeML OpenRAIL-M](./creativeml-openrail-m.md), used for SDXL and SDXL-derived models. The "++" denotes incremental amendments by Stability AI that:

1. Add Stability AI as a named contributor.
2. Tighten certain notice/attribution clauses around outputs and derivative works.
3. Otherwise preserve the use-based restrictions of the parent license.

For practical purposes, all of the **CANNOT** clauses from OpenRAIL-M (no CSAM, no discrimination, no misinformation generation, no automated legal decisions, etc.) apply identically here.
